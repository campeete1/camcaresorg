Charger Futures Vision & Plan - Initiative


This detailed draft consists of three parts. Part one of this draft will be known as Proposal 1122-1A, part two will be known as Proposal 1122-1B, and part three will be known as Proposal 1122-1C. Collectively these three parts make up the Charger Futures Vision & Plan

In part one of the Charger Futures Vision & Plan, I have selected a set of problems seen throughout our school that I believe can be changed. These problems are the lack of a modern visual announcement system or process, lack of organization of events, lack of cooperation between student-led organizations, a non-accessible centralized calendar with event dates and times, and the lack of a centralized student-led government and union.
